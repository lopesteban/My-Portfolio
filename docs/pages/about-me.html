About

I'm a software developer, born and raised in Argentina, passionate about building and designing things from scratch. I've always had a strong curiosity about how things work and I've always been persistent when it comes to solving something or fixing something broken.
I enjoy building interactive websites that look great across all platforms using mainly HTML, SASS and JavaScript. I'm always experimenting, always learning, and never bored.
I find web development a perfect blend of technical challenges and creativity, but also an exciting and challenging journey that can help anyone to learn essential things in life such as discipline and perseverance, both most valuable qualities to become a better professional.
When I'm not coding, I enjoy spending my time reading, playing soccer or hanging out with my family and friends.
